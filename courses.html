Our Courses

Medical Coding

This course introduces students to the principles and practices of medical coding. You will learn how to use the International Classification of Diseases (ICD‑10) and Current Procedural Terminology (CPT) coding systems to translate medical documentation into standardized codes for insurance claims, billing, and data analysis.

  • Fundamentals of healthcare reimbursement
  • Anatomy and medical terminology for coders
  • ICD‑10 and CPT coding guidelines
  • Regulatory compliance and ethics

Anatomy & Physiology

Gain a deep understanding of human anatomy and physiology to support accurate medical coding and clinical practice. This course covers the structure and function of the major body systems, emphasizing terminology, clinical relevance, and integration with coding protocols.

  • Introduction to cells, tissues, and organ systems
  • Detailed study of musculoskeletal, cardiovascular, respiratory, and nervous systems
  • Digestive, endocrine, and reproductive systems overview
  • Application of anatomical knowledge to medical coding
